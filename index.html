The Manny Will Not Be Televised.

This all started when @themannyspotted on TikTok created a video of the Manny who will not be televised. Following this @soupytime on TikTok proposed the idea that we all sign a petition everytime we see the Manny. So here they are, petitions to sign everytime you see the Manny who will not be televised.

With that being said, Manny is a symbol of revolution. Use him for good. The Black Lives Matter movement is a serious movement and we must treat it as such. We must all keep fighting. We must all work diligently to, not only end police brutality but, fight to bring upon the change in this racist system by any means necessary. So please, do not use the Manny that will not be televised as a joke. This is a movement that is well worth fighting for and we now have a symbol to unite us in this fight against the system.
